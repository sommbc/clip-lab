# Code Of Conduct

## Standard

Contributors are expected to keep discussion technical, respectful, and focused on improving the
project. Harassment, personal attacks, and disclosure of private data are not acceptable.

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and any project-maintained
community space.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this standard.
Repeated or severe violations may result in a block from project spaces.
